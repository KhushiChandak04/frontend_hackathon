Overview

Join us for an exciting weekend of innovation! The Innovate for Tomorrow Hackathon challenges participants to develop groundbreaking solutions focused on sustainability and environmental impact.

Whether you're a coder, designer, or strategist, bring your ideas and collaborate with others to make a difference. We'll provide mentorship, workshops, and plenty of caffeine!

Themes:

  • Renewable Energy Solutions
  • Waste Reduction & Recycling Tech
  • Sustainable Agriculture

Eligibility:

Open to all university students and young professionals globally. Must register before the deadline.

Rules & Guidelines

Please read the rules carefully...

  1. All code must be written during the hackathon duration.
  2. Teams must be between 2 and 4 members.
  3. Submissions must adhere to the specified themes.
  4. Code of Conduct must be followed at all times.
